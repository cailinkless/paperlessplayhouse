Emanuel Ramirez — "Romeo"

Curiosity. A desire to know something, a seeking. Since birth Emanuel Ramirez has been identified for his inquisitive curiousness. This has led him to find his true calling of acting.

Emanuel always knew he needed to find an environment that would give him true freedom of expression through his art. While developing his craft, and he found it at the William Esper Studio where he has mastered his voice, movement, and acting alongside his acting teacher Bruce McCarty he completed his two year acting training at this studio.

While mastering his craft Emanuel has discovered how much all the arts are connected. He has developed a love for creating, writing, producing, and acting. He believes we all have those things inside us, a creative energy to contribute something that can ultimately change the world for the better. Ultimately, he is relentlessly chasing perfection in his field, and in life knowing full well he will not catch it because nothing is perfect. But he is going to relentlessly chase it, because he believes in the process he will catch excellence.
